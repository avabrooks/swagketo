Welcome to LinkDN!

LinkDN is a networking platform where students of Del Norte High School can make profiles that allow students to connect and find common interests through similar career pathways.


We provide services such as profile creation for networking, help with résumé creation, school-wide announcements, access to school club and activities, and more!
